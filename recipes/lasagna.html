Lasagna Recipe

Description of Lasagna recipe

This type of lasagna is referred to as zucchini lasagna.This Zucchini Lasagna is low fat, gluten-free, Keto friendly, ridiculously delicious and only 368 calories! This lasagna uses zucchini in place of pasta thereby reducing calories. Guilt free!

Ingredients

  • cooking spray
  • 1 ½ large zucchinis, thinly sliced lengthwise
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 ½ cups low-carb marinara sauce
  • 2 teaspoons salt, divided
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 (8 ounce) container ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • ½ teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • aluminum foil
